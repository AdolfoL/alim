Mango flan

Ingredients

200 grs. peeled mango, 200 ml. of skim milk, 200 ml. of cream for cooking (15% fat), 6 sheets of gelatin (if you can not find them, you can use neutral gelatin powder, the recommended amount for 500 ml, which is usually an envelope and follow the manufacturer's instructions to add it, 1 spoonful of vanilla sugar, 3 tablespoons of white sugar (about 60 grams), liquid caramel.

Preparation

Caramelize the mold that you prefer, since not having to cook it will serve us any.

You can use packaged liquid caramel or do it yourself.

You just have to warm a few seconds to the micro at low power (if the container is suitable), to make it more liquid and thus be able to impregnate the walls by tilting the mold.

If it was metallic, put in another container with hot water until you see that it gets well fluid.

Add to a nonstick skillet about 4 tablespoons of sugar and a little water.

Let it begin to make bubbles and then stir with the same pan until it has a dark tone.

Heat the glass of milk, the two types of sugar and the chopped mango in a saucepan without boiling, while soaking the gelatine leaves in cold water.

When the contents of the saucepan is hot add the leaves of gelatin squeezed with your hands and stir a few seconds (dissolve at once), remove the saucepan from the heat and grind well with the blender.

Add the cream and stir with a spatula or spoon until you see that it has been integrated with the rest of the mixture, pour everything into the mold, passing through a colander if there are remains of mango, once tempered, let cool in the refrigerator or refrigerator about 4-5 hours if it is a large mold and if the molds are individual you will have it ready in less time, if you use a silicone mold, place a plate underneath to facilitate the transfer to the refrigerator without being deformed; and unmold by doing a little pressure with the fingers from the edges towards the center around the contour, in other molds a fine knife passes the edges.

Custard cream and coconut in microwave

Ingredients

4 eggs, 100 gr. of sugar (we have used fructose), 300 ml. of semi-skimmed milk, 200 ml. of cream (milk cream) 35% fat, 100 gr. of coconut, 100 gr. of sugar or fructose for liquid candy.

Preparation

In a saucepan, put 100 grams of sugar and not very strong fire let it caramelize. When you have the desired color, remove and pour in a microwave mold.

In a bowl, we beat the eggs and 100 gr. of sugar with manual rods. Add milk and cream and mix well.

Pour the mixture into the mold we have with the caramelised sugar and cook ten minutes in the microwave at 60% power. And six more minutes at 80%.

Times may vary depending on the microwave power. We will know what is done when we use a toothpick to remove it clean.

Let cool to room temperature and keep in the fridge.
