Apple cookies

Ingredients

120 g of sugar, 1 sachet of vanillin (vanilla pod), 4 eggs, 200 g of margarine, 500 g of flour, 1 sachet of chemical yeast, 3 apples.

Preparation

Beat the eggs with the sugar and vanillin, add the melted margarine, mix well.

Add the flour mixed with the chemical yeast and mix with the spoon.

Peel the apples, chopped into small cubes and add them to the dough.

Form piles with the spoon on vegetable paper placed on the baking tray and take it to the preheated oven at 200 ° С for about 10-15 minutes, until the biscuits are a little browned.

Preparation

Heat the oven to 180 ° С.

Line the oven tray with greaseproof paper.

Chop all the chocolate.

With the blender mix the eggs with the sugars for about 4 minutes.

Melt 240 g of chocolate with all the butter in a water bath or in the microwave.

Add the melted chocolate to the egg mixture with sugar and beat at low speed.

In a bowl mix the flour, the chemical yeast and the salt. Add the result to the chocolate mixture and stir.

Add the 150 grams of chopped chocolate and stir.

Spoon piles of dough on vegetable paper placed on the baking tray, leaving about 5-7 cm between them.
