Classic Bagels

Ingredientes

For the first mass:

550 ml of hot water, 1 teaspoon instant dry yeast, 450 g of bread flour.

For the second mass:

¾ teaspoon instant dry yeast, 60 g of honey, 375 g of bread flour, 1 tablespoon of salt, 1 l of water, 5 g of baking soda.

For dressings:

2 tablespoons of honey, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, 1 lightly whipped egg white, sunflower seeds, sesame seeds, onion flakes.

Preparation

First, prepare the dough. We put the water in a bowl. We add 3 grams of yeast and 450 g of flour and mix with a tablespoon of wood.

Cover the dough and let it rest for at least 10 minutes.

After the time, we add to the dough 3 more grams of yeast, 30 g of honey, flour and salt. We knead slowly for about 7 minutes.

Let the dough rest at room temperature for 1 hour. Then, we put it in the fridge and let it cool throughout the night.

We make the bagels and let them rest, covered with a cloth, for 15 minutes.

Then, we boil water, 30 g of honey and baking soda. We submerge them with a skimmer one by one and cook them for 90 seconds to 2 and a half minutes.

We preheat the oven to a temperature of 220ºC.

Before the bagels go to the oven, we prepare the dressings.

We beat the egg white and paint with it the surface of the bagels. Sprinkle some with sesame seeds, others with poppy seeds and others with onion flakes and seeds.

We bake the bagels for 20 minutes during 220ºC. Then, turn off the oven, open the door and let them sit for 10 minutes, Serve.

SPICE BREAD

Ingredients

400 g of sugar, 200 g of honey, 250 g of milk, 300 g of rye flour, 40 g of impeller, 250 g of eggs, 30 g of powdered spices (pepper, cloves, ginger and nutmeg).

Preparation

We preheat the oven to 180ºC.

First, in a large bowl, mix the eggs, sugar, spices and honey.

On the other hand, in another bowl, we mix the flours and the impeller.

We add the dry ingredients to the main bowl and stir well. We add the milk and we mix everything with enveloping movements.

Pour the mixture into a greased mold and bake 30 minutes at 180ºC.

After this time, we let temper and serve with the pate of livers to the brandy.
