Chocolate truffles with condensed milk

Ingredients

1 tin of condensed milk (approximately 400 g), 2 tablespoons of 100% pure cocoa powder, 1 tablespoon of butter, 1 pinch of salt (optional), 100% pure cocoa powder to batter.

Preparation

Place the butter in a pan or saucepan over low heat and wait for it to melt. Next, add the salt and cocoa powder and mix until all the lumps are dissolved. Salt helps accentuate the taste of cocoa, but it is an optional ingredient.

Add the condensed milk and stir until the mixture is removed from the bottom of the pan as seen in the photo. At that time, turn off the heat and transfer the mixture to a buttered mold.

Let the mixture cool and form the balls of condensed milk and chocolate with your hands, roughly shape the walnut-sized truffles.

Place the remaining cocoa powder in a dish and coat the chocolate truffles made with condensed milk until completely covered. And ready! Enjoy this sweet with pure cocoa and tell us in the comments what has appeared.

Chocolate frappe

Ingredients

500 ml.of almond milk, 500 ml.of coconut milk powder (without sugar), 100gr.of brown sugar, 1 teaspoon of cinnamon, Vanilla essence to taste (optional).

Place the ice cubes in the glass of a powerful blender or in the picador accessory of the same and water with the coconut milk.

Grind until both ingredients are completely integrated and the ice is completely chopped.

Add the chocolate powder, cinnamon and brown sugar. Continue grinding until all the ingredients are completely integrated.

To finish, add the essence of vanilla and mix everything very well.

Remove the glasses from the freezer and distribute the chocolate and cinnamon frappe on them.
