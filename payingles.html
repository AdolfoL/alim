Rice pudding with milk

Ingredients

For the scab:

2 cups crushed cinnamon cookies, 1/4 cup melted butter.

For the filling:

1 cup of rice soaked in hot water, 2 cups of whole milk, 1 1/2 cups of evaporated milk, 1/2 cup of condensed milk, 2 tablespoons of vanilla, 1/2 cup of brown sugar, Â½ cup of raisins, 2 slices of cinnamon, 1 tablespoon of natural grenetine hydrated, Cinnamon powder.

Preparation

Mix the ground cookies with the butter until you get a mass that cakes.

Add the cookie mixture to a mold and flatten with a uniform base.

Refrigerate the mold at least 1 hour or until the base has hardened.

Heat the whole milk in a saucepan, evaporated and condensed; add sugar, rice, cinnamon slit and vanilla essence.

Cook for 30 minutes and add the raisins; Finish cooking the rice for 10 more minutes or until the rice is soft. Remove the cinnamon slices.

Blend half of the preparation with the hydrated gelatin, transfer to another container and add the rest of the rice.

Add the rice mixture and pour over the biscuit base. Refrigerate for 2 hours or until the pie sets.

Serve and decorate with cinnamon.

Lemon juice

Ingredients

2 tubes of Maria biscuit, 1 can of evaporated milk, 1 can of condensed milk, 6 lemons.

Preparation

Squeeze the lemons to have the lemon juice without seeds.

Blend evaporated milk, condensed milk and lemon until a cream is left.

In a refractory or mold, arrange a layer of cookies as a base and add the previously prepared cream and repeat the process until you reach the top of the container.

Refrigerate for 2 hours and serve.
